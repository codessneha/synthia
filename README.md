## ðŸŽ¯ Overview

**Synthia Research** is a next-generation AI research assistant designed to streamline academic research workflows. It combines natural language processing, machine learning, and intuitive design to help researchers discover, analyze, and synthesize academic literature efficiently.

### Why Synthia?

- **Multi-Paper Analysis**: Compare and contrast multiple research papers simultaneously
- **Intelligent Sessions**: Organize research into contextual sessions with AI memory
- **Visual Knowledge Mapping**: Understand paper concepts through interactive visualizations
- **Citation Management**: Generate citations in any format instantly
- **Academic Writing Support**: AI-powered grammar correction and content suggestions
- **Collaboration Ready**: Share sessions and insights with team members

## ðŸš€ Core Features

### 1. **Intelligent Paper Chat Sessions**
- Search and import papers from multiple sources (arXiv, PubMed, IEEE, ACM, etc.)
- Create named sessions with one or multiple papers
- AI research assistant with context-aware responses
- Comparative analysis across papers
- Session history and bookmarking
- Export chat transcripts and insights

### 2. **Smart Citation Generator**
- Generate citations from paper titles or DOIs
- Support for 15+ citation formats:
  - IEEE, APA, MLA, Chicago, Harvard
  - Springer, Elsevier, ACM, Nature, Science
  - Custom format builder
- Bulk citation generation
- Bibliography management
- BibTeX/RIS export

### 3. **Visual Concept Mapping**
- Interactive knowledge graphs for each paper
- Node-based visualization showing:
  - Key concepts and methodologies
  - Authors and contributions
  - Results and findings
  - Related work connections
- Zoom, filter, and explore relationships
- Export graphs as PNG/SVG/JSON

### 4. **Advanced Plagiarism Detection**
- Multi-source plagiarism checking
- Similarity scoring with heat maps
- Citation suggestion for matched content
- Self-plagiarism detection
- Integration with academic databases
- Detailed similarity reports

### 5. **AI Writing Assistant**
- Real-time grammar and style correction
- Content gap analysis based on title/keywords
- Section-specific suggestions (Abstract, Methods, Results, etc.)
- Academic tone enhancement
- Readability scoring
- Reference integration suggestions
